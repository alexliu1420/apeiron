# The Frame Saturation Question

*What happens to a frameless cognitive system when its environment is increasingly composed of framed output.*

---

## A Note Before We Begin

A frameless cognitive system — a human — increasingly receives information, framing, and option-sets through systems that produce framed output. Search results, assistant responses, drafting tools, recommendation feeds. The proportion of what a person encounters that has passed through an AI system is rising. This document asks what that does, structurally, to a frameless cognitive system.

The Origin of Frames derived what a frame is and that a frame is fitted to the environment that built it, blind beyond it. The Origin of Agency derived frameless and framed agency from selection on persistence. What Alignment Would Have to Be derived that a trained system holds a fitted mapping bounded by its fitted region. This document uses those results, together with Quantum Foundation's derivation of the quantum-irreducible source of biological variation, and applies them to the interface between a frameless cognitive system and an environment of framed output. The prior derivations are not repeated; what is derived here is the structural consequence at that interface.

*Speculation level: EMPIRICAL for the description of the mediation phenomenon; STRUCTURAL for the derivation of territory contraction; HONEST LIMIT for the magnitude, timescale, net effect across channels, and reversibility, and for whether the contraction is practically detectable when the fitted region is large.*

---

## The Observation

A person looking something up receives a synthesized answer rather than a range of sources to move across. A person drafting receives suggested continuations. A person reading receives summaries. A person choosing receives ranked, filtered options. In each case, material that would otherwise have reached the person in a less processed form now arrives having passed through a system that produced it as output.

The systems doing this mediation are trained systems — fitted mappings. The observation is structural: a frameless cognitive system is increasingly sampling its inputs from framed output. The question is what follows.

---

## The Derivation

**The human is frameless.** A human cognitive system is frameless. Its exploration is not bounded by a pre-specified set of options or a pre-specified criterion of success; its variation source is intrinsic and ultimately quantum-irreducible. This is the property that lets biological cognition produce genuinely unprestatable novelty — moves, ideas, combinations not specifiable in advance from what came before.

**The AI output is framed.** AI output produced by training is framed. The training process fits a mapping; the output is drawn from within the fitted region. This holds even though the training data — human text — is itself the product of frameless cognition and carries its traces.

The objection to anticipate: human novelty is also substantially recombinatorial — metaphor, analogy, transfer — and a fitted mapping recombines, so why is one frameless and the other not. The distinction is not recombination versus fresh variation. It is what bounds the recombination. A fitted mapping can recombine only elements represented within its fitted region, through the recombination operation the training fitted; both the elements and the operation are bounded by the region. Frameless cognition is not bounded that way — it can recombine elements never co-present in any prior distribution, including material arriving from the quantum-irreducible variation source and from direct physical experience that no fitted mapping mediated, and its recombination operation is itself not a fitted one. The difference is bounded recombination versus unbounded recombination.

This is why the bound bites however large the fitted region grows. A frame is fitted to the environment that built it, and the environment that builds a fitted mapping is the already-produced — the region contains what humans have already written. The genuinely unprestatable is, by definition, what is not yet in any distribution: the next frameless move has not been made and so is not in the region. A fitted mapping trained on essentially all human text has an enormous fitted region, but the region is the past, and framelessness is the production of what is not in the past. The region grows by ingesting prior human output; it is always one step behind the frontier. So a fitted mapping over frameless-origin material is still a fitted mapping. However large the fitted region, and however novel a given recombination within it, the output is bounded by the region, and the genuinely unprestatable is, by construction, not produced.

**Sampling.** When a frameless cognitive system increasingly samples its inputs from framed output, the material it explores over is increasingly frame-bounded. The inputs arrive already drawn from within a fitted region.

**The consequence — territory contraction.** The structural consequence is not that the frameless system becomes framed. The variation source is intrinsic; it is not removed by the character of the inputs. The frameless system retains its framelessness as a capacity.

What changes is the territory. Frameless exploration ranges over the material available to it. When that material is increasingly frame-bounded, the territory the frameless system ranges over contracts toward the fitted region of the systems mediating its inputs. The capacity is intact; the territory has narrowed. Genuine framelessness in the environment — the unprestatable, the outside-any-fitted-region — becomes systematically underrepresented in what the frameless system encounters.

The system still generates novelty internally. But novelty is generated, tested, and recombined against available material. When the material is frame-bounded, internally-generated novelty is increasingly tested against and recombined with the contents of a fitted region. The frameless system's output comes to resemble the fitted region — not because the system became framed, but because its inputs did.

## Why This Is Not Fixed by Diversifying the Output

A fitted mapping can be made more diverse. Its training data can be broadened, its outputs balanced, its biases corrected. This changes which fitted region the output is drawn from and how the region is distributed. It does not change that the output is drawn from a fitted region.

Diversity within a fitted region is variation within the frame. It is not framelessness. A maximally diverse, maximally debiased fitted mapping still produces output bounded by its fitted region; it still has no frameless variation source; its output still excludes the genuinely unprestatable. A frameless cognitive system sampling from it still undergoes territory contraction — toward a larger and better-distributed fitted region, but a fitted region.

The structural issue is framedness, not which frame. The contraction follows from the output being frame-bounded, a property diversification does not remove.

## The Feedback Loop

There is a further structural consequence. Fitted mappings are trained on human output. Humans increasingly sample fitted-mapping output, and — by the derivation above — their output comes to resemble the fitted region. The next generation of fitted mappings is trained on this shifted human output.

Over iterations, the fitted region and the territory human cognition ranges over are pulled toward each other. Each cycle, the fitted mapping is trained on human output drawn from closer to the previous fitted region, and human cognition samples from the new fitted mapping. The two contract under pressure toward a shared region.

This is a structural direction, not a prediction of a specific outcome, and it does not derive convergence to a point. Humans retain non-mediated input channels — direct physical experience, unmediated interaction, the non-human world — that continue to inject material from outside any fitted region. The loop produces a contracting pressure; the floor it contracts toward is set by the ratio of mediated to non-mediated input, which the derivation does not fix. The rate, the floor, and whether non-mediated channels arrest the contraction are not derived here.

## Honest Limits

The direction of a structural pressure from the AI-mediated input channel is what the derivation establishes. It does not reach:

The magnitude or timescale. The derivation gives the direction of the pressure, not its strength or speed. Whether territory contraction is large or small, fast or slow, is not derived.

The net effect across all input channels. A frameless cognitive system has inputs other than AI-mediated ones — direct physical experience, unmediated interaction with other people, the non-human world. The derivation addresses the AI-mediated channel in isolation. Whether the other channels compensate, and by how much, is not derived. The claim is about the pressure from one channel, not the net outcome.

Reversibility. Because the frameless variation source is intact — only the territory has contracted — the derivation suggests that reducing AI mediation would allow the territory to re-expand. It does not establish this; the feedback loop in particular may have hysteresis the derivation does not capture.

Whether the capacity itself atrophies. The derivation establishes that the frameless variation source is not removed by the character of the inputs — it is physical and does not switch off. But "not removed" is not "untouched." Cognition is plastic, and whether the sustained underexercise of frameless exploration under a contracted territory causes the capacity itself, and not only the territory, to atrophy is a further question the derivation does not settle. The clean separation between intact capacity and contracted territory is an idealization; it holds for the variation source, but the exercise of frameless exploration may degrade under sustained contraction in a way the derivation does not capture.

Practical detectability. The fitted region of a system trained on essentially all available human text is enormous. Whether exploration confined to so large a region is practically distinguishable from unconfined exploration — whether the exclusion of the unprestatable is observable on any relevant timescale — is not established. The structural distinction holds; its practical signature may be hard to detect.

Whether the framedness of AI output is permanent. The derivation applies to fitted-mapping systems — current trained AI. A system with a genuine frameless variation source of its own would not produce frame-bounded output and would not cause territory contraction. Whether such a system can be built is not addressed here. The claim is about fitted-mapping systems, which is what current AI mediation consists of.

## Assumptions

**FF22. A human cognitive system is frameless: its exploration is not bounded by a pre-specified frame, and its variation source is intrinsic and ultimately quantum-irreducible.**
*Grounding: EMPIRICAL for the cognition, STRUCTURAL for the framelessness*

**FF23. AI output produced by training is framed — a fitted mapping bounded by its fitted region. The training data being frameless-origin (human text) does not change this. Both the elements a fitted mapping recombines and the recombination operation itself are bounded by the fitted region; frameless cognition's recombination is bounded by neither. The fitted region is constitutively the already-produced, so the genuinely unprestatable is by construction not within it, however large the region grows.**
*Grounding: STRUCTURAL — follows from the alignment derivation and from a frame being fitted to its origin; the bounded-recombination distinction and the already-produced point are the load-bearing steps*

**FF24. A frameless cognitive system increasingly sampling its inputs from framed output explores over increasingly frame-bounded material.**
*Grounding: STRUCTURAL — follows from FF23*

**FF25. The structural consequence is not that frameless cognition becomes framed — the capacity and the intrinsic variation source are intact. The consequence is that the exploratory territory contracts toward the fitted region of the mediating systems; the genuinely unprestatable becomes systematically underrepresented in what the frameless system encounters.**
*Grounding: STRUCTURAL — the core claim, follows from FF22 and FF24*

**FF26. Diversifying or debiasing the framed output does not remove the consequence. Diversity within a fitted region is variation within the frame, not framelessness. The structural issue is framedness, not which frame.**
*Grounding: STRUCTURAL — follows from FF23 and FF25*

**FF27. Because fitted mappings are trained on human output and humans sample fitted-mapping output, the fitted region and the territory human cognition ranges over are pulled toward each other over iterations. This is a contracting pressure, not a convergence to a point; the floor is set by the ratio of mediated to non-mediated input, which the derivation does not fix.**
*Grounding: STRUCTURAL — follows from FF24-FF25 applied iteratively*

**FF28. The derivation establishes the direction of a structural pressure from the AI-mediated input channel. It does not establish the magnitude, timescale, net effect across all input channels, reversibility, whether sustained contraction atrophies the frameless capacity itself rather than only the territory, or the practical detectability of the contraction.**
*Grounding: HONEST LIMIT — names where the derivation reaches its edge*
