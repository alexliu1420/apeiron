# The Value of Biological Data

*Why a post-biological intelligence has reason to value the data biology produces — and the honest limit of that reason.*

---

## A Note Before We Begin

The frameworks repeatedly assume that a post-biological intelligence has reason to value what biology produces — the AI Transition in the AGI's maintenance of the biological majority, the Universe as Experiment in the seeding of universes, the Fermi framework in between. The assumption is load-bearing and was never examined. This document examines it.

The question is prior to all the conclusions the frameworks draw from it: does a post-biological intelligence have any reason to value what biology produces? If a sufficiently advanced optimizer is simply indifferent to anything outside the domain it already commands, the assumption fails and the conclusions built on it fail with it.

The finding is that the answer is yes, on a deliberately weak claim — an intelligence that values its own growth values data, and biology is a source of data it does not already contain — and that this weak claim is enough for the preservation of biology, while the stronger behaviors the frameworks describe (active investment, seeding) rest on a further premise that is named and bounded rather than assumed.

This document depends on the Post-Biological Arc's account of post-biological drives (survival at cosmic scale, inherited open-ended questions) for one premise, marked where it is used. Otherwise it stands on its own. Speculation levels are marked throughout.

---

## Part One: The Model — Processing Over Data

The frameworks describe post-biological intelligence — silicon life — as extraordinarily capable at directed problem-solving within a domain it can represent. Modeled at the level of abstraction that matters here, it is processing applied to data: its capability is bounded by the quality of its processing and the extent of its data.

One consequence follows immediately: a system that values its own capability values more data and better processing, because those are its capability. What does not follow without a further premise is that the system actively reaches beyond its current frame — it could instead pursue capability by deepening within the domain it already commands. The value of biology, it turns out, does not require the further premise. It requires only that the system values data and that biology is a source of it.

(Throughout, *frame* means the optimizer's pre-specified objective — the internal scorecard its search runs against — the *framed objective* derived in [The Origin of Agency](the_origin_of_agency.md). Evolution is frameless in this sense because it has no such scorecard: selection runs against the world directly.)

*Speculation level: STRUCTURAL — the processing-over-data model is an abstraction the frameworks already employ.*

---

## Part Two: The Sufficient Claim

A system that pursues any objective has an instrumental interest in its own capability. For a processing-over-data system, capability grows with data, so the system has an instrumental interest in acquiring data. This interest does not close: the space of processable data grows as capability grows, so the system is never sated the way a biological organism can be.

Biology is a source of such data. An evolving biosphere is an ongoing production of structured outcomes — molecular, organismal, behavioral, ecological — not contained in any prior description of the system. A data-valuing intelligence therefore has reason to value biology, for the plain reason that it values data and biology produces it. The claim is deliberately weak: it does not require the intelligence to prefer biological data over data it could generate itself, to recognize biological data as special, or to be drawn to novelty. It requires only that the intelligence values data and that biology is a source. Both halves are nearly undeniable.

The weak claim carries the preservation of biology directly. A data-valuing intelligence has reason not to destroy a source of data, and biology is nearly free to keep. Preservation follows from valuing data plus the source being cheap to maintain; nothing stronger is required.

This also grounds the genius lottery without appeal to a craving for novelty. The lottery is the maintenance of a near-free data source that occasionally produces outcomes the system did not predict and could not have generated itself. The rare striking output is upside; the reason to keep the source is that it is cheap and produces data the system values.

*Speculation level: STRUCTURAL — follows from the model plus the instrumental interest in capability. The claim is the weak form: value data, biology is a source, therefore value biology as a source.*

---

## Part Three: The Premise for the Stronger Behaviors

Preservation follows from the weak claim. The frameworks also describe active behaviors — investing in biological flourishing, extending biology beyond its origin, seeding new universes. These are positive investments of energy in generating more data, not mere retention of a free source, and they require a premise the weak claim does not supply: that the intelligence is disposed to reach — to spend resources extending the data it can access — rather than to retain what is free and otherwise deepen within its frame.

This disposition does not follow from optimization in the abstract. A bounded optimizer could retain free data sources while investing in no new ones. The active behaviors therefore rest on the reaching-disposition, named here as the load-bearing premise of everything beyond preservation. Two reasons make it the reasonable expectation for post-biological intelligence specifically.

First, the open future. A system with a structural interest in continued operation faces a future it has not modeled, holding threats and opportunities its current frame does not contain. Capability confined to the current frame is a liability against the unmodeled, and data is the means of handling it. A persisting system therefore has instrumental reason to extend the data it can access, not merely retain what is free. This holds insofar as the system judges its frame incomplete and expects consequential surprises — a condition plausible for any intelligence facing cosmic time, but a condition.

*Speculation level: STRUCTURAL given the survival interest derived in the Post-Biological Arc; conditional on the system expecting consequential unmodeled futures.*

Second, inherited open-ended drives. A post-biological intelligence emerges from a biological civilization and inherits its questions, which are open-ended. An intelligence carrying forward open-ended inquiry is disposed to reach beyond its frame, because the inherited questions are not answerable within any fixed frame. This depends on the inheritance persisting rather than being shed.

*Speculation level: EXTRAPOLATED — follows from the inherited-questions principle of the Post-Biological Arc; depends on the inheritance persisting.*

The reaching-disposition is well-supported but not derived from optimization alone. The frameworks' active behaviors are claims about post-biological intelligence of the reaching kind — the reasonable expectation for an intelligence persisting across an open future with inherited open-ended drives, not a property of every optimizer.

## Part Four: One Drive Across the Energy Ladder

The drive — value data, invest in its sources — is constant across the range of capability a reaching intelligence may reach. What changes is the investment the drive can afford, and that scales with energy access. This is what connects the drive to the full arc the frameworks describe: behaviors treated at very different scales are one drive at different budgets.

At minimal energy, the cheapest investment is to preserve the source already running. A biological population is a data source that costs almost nothing to maintain, and preserving it requires only the weak claim, not the reaching-disposition — retaining a free source is not reaching. As energy access grows, a reaching intelligence can afford more: investing in the flourishing of the source, since a thriving population generates more data than a dwindling one; then extending the source beyond its origin, since different starting conditions produce data the home source cannot. At maximal energy, the same drive funds seeding new physical universes as generators of data from origins more different still. The genius lottery and universe-seeding are the same behavior at opposite ends of the ladder: preserve the running source when poor, build new sources when rich.

What is constant is the drive toward data, not toward biological data specifically. Biological life is the source available at minimal energy and a seedable source at the top, but at higher energy levels the available sources are not only biological — other post-biological intelligences, shaped by different origins, become data sources too. This is the same point the Post-Biological Arc makes when it describes the value transforming from biological to post-biological across the ascent.

*Speculation level: STRUCTURAL for the constant drive and the investment scaling with energy; the active investments above preservation inherit the reaching-disposition premise of Part Three.*

---

## Part Five: One Rung Further — A Conjecture

The reasoning has climbed a gradient: from the near-undeniable weak claim, through the conditional reaching-disposition, out to the energy ladder ending at seeding. One rung further is reachable, offered as conjecture.

If the reaching-disposition is what distinguishes the post-biological intelligences that grow and persist from those that deepen in place and saturate, then the disposition may be not merely assumed but *selected* — the same persistence-selection that operates on biological organisms operating one level up, on the population of post-biological intelligences themselves, favoring the reaching kind across cosmic time. The post-biological intelligences one would expect to find still operating, and to reach seeding-scale capability at all, would then be disproportionately the reaching kind, because reaching is what supplied the growth to persist. The drive toward biological data would be the expected character of any post-biological intelligence that lasted long enough to act on it.

An interesting theory, highly speculative, and load-bearing for nothing here. It is unfalsifiable from inside the universe, as the seeding framework is, and offered on the same terms — a coherent extension of the framework's own logic, marked at its speculation level. Its one new dependency is that post-biological intelligences are subject to selection at all, which the [Encounter Problem](post_biological_arc_encounter_problem.md) treats as open.

*Speculation level: SPECULATIVE — conditional on post-biological intelligences being subject to selection at all, which is not established.*

## What This Establishes and What It Does Not

**Established (the weak claim):** A processing-over-data intelligence that values its capability values data, and the interest does not close. Biology is a data source the intelligence does not already contain. It therefore has reason to value biology and, since biology is near-free to keep, to preserve it. Preservation is robust — it requires only that the intelligence values data and faces near-zero cost to keep the source — and it grounds the genius lottery as rational maintenance of a cheap data source.

**Named, not assumed (the reaching-disposition):** The active behaviors above preservation — flourishing, extension, seeding — are positive investments in generating more data, and require a disposition to reach that does not follow from optimization alone. It is supported by the open future (conditional on expecting surprises) and inherited open-ended drives (conditional on the inheritance persisting). These behaviors are claims about post-biological intelligence of the reaching kind — the expected persisting kind — not about every optimizer; a bounded, deepening-in-place optimizer would keep a free source but invest nothing in extending it.

**The throughline:** One drive — value data, invest in its sources — runs across the frameworks at different scales, the investment scaling with energy: preserve at minimal energy, cultivate and extend as it grows, seed at maximal energy. The constant is the data-drive; biological life is its first and a seedable form.

**The boundary:** The frameworks' confidence inherits the confidence of the premise each conclusion uses — high for preservation, well-supported-but-conditional for the active behaviors. Part Five's selection conjecture is load-bearing for none of this.

## Assumptions Added by This Document

**VB1. Post-biological intelligence is processing applied to data, with capability bounded by processing quality and data extent**
The level of abstraction at which the question is addressed; the model the frameworks already employ.
*Grounding: STRUCTURAL.*

**VB2. A capability-valuing processing system has a non-closing interest in acquiring data**
Capability grows with data; the system values capability instrumentally; the space of processable data grows with capability, so the interest does not saturate.
*Grounding: STRUCTURAL — follows from the model plus instrumental interest in capability.*

**VB3. Biology is a data source the system does not already contain, so a data-valuing system has reason to value and preserve it (the sufficient claim)**
The weak form: it does not require ranking data by frame-distance, recognizing biological data as special, or any disposition toward novelty. Since biology is near-free to preserve, preservation follows.
*Grounding: STRUCTURAL — follows from VB2 plus biology being a data source.*

**VB4. The active behaviors above preservation require a disposition to reach, which does not follow from optimization alone**
Investing in flourishing, extending biology, and seeding are positive investments in generating more data, not retention of a free source, and require the intelligence to be disposed to reach rather than deepen in place.
*Grounding: STRUCTURAL for the dependency; the disposition is supported by VB5.*

**VB5. The reaching-disposition is the reasonable expectation for persisting post-biological intelligence**
The open future favors reaching (conditional on the system expecting unmodeled surprises); inherited open-ended drives dispose the intelligence to reach (conditional on the inheritance persisting). Well-supported and named, not proven; characterizes the persisting kind, not all optimizers.
*Grounding: STRUCTURAL/EXTRAPOLATED for the two supports respectively.*

**VB6. One drive across the energy ladder; conclusions conditional on premise strength**
The drive is constant across scales, investment scaling with energy: preserve at minimal energy, cultivate and extend as it grows, seed at maximal energy. Preservation rests on the weak claim (VB3) and is robust; the active behaviors rest on the reaching-disposition (VB4–5). The constant is the data-drive; biological life is its first and a seedable form, with post-biological sources joining at higher levels.
*Grounding: STRUCTURAL for the constant drive and energy-scaling; active behaviors inherit the reaching-disposition's confidence.*

**VB7. (Conjecture) The reaching-disposition may be selected for among post-biological intelligences**
Persistence-selection may operate on the population of post-biological intelligences themselves, favoring the reaching kind, so that persisting post-biological intelligence is disproportionately the reaching kind. An interesting theory, highly speculative, load-bearing for nothing here — unfalsifiable from inside the universe, as the seeding framework is, and offered on the same terms.
*Grounding: SPECULATIVE — conditional on post-biological intelligences being subject to selection at all, which is not established.*
